# Gambling Law Summary Reference Pack

Detailed drafting rules for `.claude/skills/gambling-law-summary/SKILL.md`. Load this pack only when drafting, tailoring, or quality-checking a gambling-law regulatory summary.

## Output Template

### Header

Include title, jurisdiction(s), effective date range, prepared date, and audience.

### Executive Summary

Use 5-12 bullets covering:

- Legal status by vertical.
- Primary regulator(s) and market entry barriers.
- High-risk compliance obligations and tax/fee exposure.
- Active enforcement priorities and pending reforms.

### Scope Table

| Item | Detail |
|---|---|

| Sources | Statutes, regs, agency guidance, case law |

### Legal Status and Authority

- Identify permitted, prohibited, and restricted categories with statutory basis.
- Explain preemption hierarchy.
- Include tribal framework and compact status if applicable.

### Regulatory Bodies

- Identify primary and secondary regulators.
- Summarize licensing board composition and powers where relevant.

### Citation and Disclaimer

- Use Bluebook format where appropriate.
- Hyperlink official sources.
- Mark uncertain items `[Unverified]`.
- Include disclaimer: gambling law changes rapidly; summary is informational and not legal advice.

## Pitfalls

- Never blend federal, state, and tribal regimes; separate each authority clearly.
- Always flag IGRA, compacts, or federal preemption issues when relevant.
- Use only primary sources or regulator guidance for legal status assertions.
- Include effective dates and amendment history for all cited authorities.
- Label uncertainty explicitly and avoid speculative conclusions.
- Keep tone executive-ready and compliance-focused.
