# Game-Regulation Issue Taxonomy

Use this taxonomy to preserve game-specific issue spotting in merged mode.

## Product Classification

- game
- game platform
- distribution platform
- marketplace
- user-generated content service

Typical questions:

- Is the product regulated as a game, gambling product, digital content service,
  payment service, platform, or mixed service?
- Is a local publisher, license, or content classification needed before launch?
- Which regulator has primary jurisdiction and which bodies are adjacent?

## Randomized Rewards

- loot boxes
- gacha
- paid/free distinction
- probability disclosure
- randomized reward advertising

Issue prompts:

- Is the randomized mechanic paid, free, or indirectly paid through premium
  currency?
- Are probabilities disclosed in-game, on the website, and at the point required
  by local rules?
- Are probability changes versioned and auditable?
- Is the issue framed as gambling, consumer protection, game-specific disclosure,
  youth protection, or self-regulation?
- Are enforcement materials available from game, consumer, gambling, or fair
  trade regulators?

## Minors and Youth Protection

- age ratings
- harmful content classification
- play-time limits
- parental consent
- minor payment or refund issues

Issue prompts:

- Does the game need a rating before distribution?
- Are spending limits, play-time limits, real-name checks, or parental consent
  requirements triggered?
- Are ads or monetization mechanics directed at minors?
- Are refund or cancellation rules stricter for minors?

## Consumer Protection

- refunds
- unfair terms
- subscription cancellation
- digital content defects
- withdrawal/cooling-off limits

Issue prompts:

- Are virtual goods treated as digital content, service credits, payment
  instruments, or something else?
- Are refund exclusions enforceable?
- Are probability, scarcity, discount, or limited-time claims misleading?
- Are unfair terms, unilateral changes, or account termination issues present?

## Advertising and Monetization

- influencer marketing
- dark patterns
- in-app purchase disclosures
- probability or scarcity claims
- event reward disclosures

Issue prompts:

- Is sponsorship or influencer compensation clearly disclosed?
- Are price, scarcity, countdown, bundle, or event claims substantiated?
- Are minors targeted?
- Are in-app purchases and paid currency disclosed before payment?

## Virtual Goods and Currency

- paid currency
- exchangeability
- transferability
- cash-out restrictions
- gambling boundary

Issue prompts:

- Can users redeem, transfer, sell, or cash out the item or currency?
- Does the design create gambling, payment, virtual-asset, or money-transmission
  risk?
- Are secondary markets tolerated, blocked, or operated by the publisher?

## Privacy/Data Handoff

- child data
- telemetry
- profiling
- cross-border transfer
- account linking
- ad targeting

Use this category for spotting and handoff when a data-protection specialist is
running.

Issue prompts:

- What data facts should be handed to the privacy specialist?
- Are minors, profiling, targeted ads, or cross-border transfers involved?
- Does the game-law analysis depend on data-protection conclusions?

## Platform and Distribution

- app-store policies with legal implications
- payment rules
- local publishing requirements
- intermediary/platform obligations

Issue prompts:

- Are app-store rules merely commercial terms, or do they interact with local
  law?
- Is local registration, local partner publishing, or content approval required?
- Are platform fee, billing, or anti-steering issues legally regulated?

## Enforcement and Remedies

- regulator
- sanctions
- corrective orders
- administrative fines
- criminal exposure
- private remedies

Issue prompts:

- Which regulator can investigate?
- Are sanctions administrative, civil, criminal, or self-regulatory?
- Are corrective orders, disclosure updates, refunds, takedowns, or product
  modifications likely remedies?
- Is there recent enforcement practice or only theoretical statutory exposure?
