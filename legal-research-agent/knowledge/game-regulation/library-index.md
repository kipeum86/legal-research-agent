## Korean Primary Source Targets

Verify current official law before citing:

- Game Industry Promotion Act and delegated rules.
- E-Commerce Consumer Protection Act where digital content/refunds matter.
- Youth Protection Act where minors matter.
- Telecommunications Business Act where app-market/payment rules matter.
- Monopoly Regulation and Fair Trade Act where platform fees or abuse of
  dominance matters.
- Copyright Act where game IP/copying matters.
- PIPA only for spotting unless the data-protection specialist is not running.
