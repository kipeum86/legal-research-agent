# Game Industry Regulatory Map

Use this compact map during jurisdiction planning. Verify all authority claims
against current official sources before citing.

## Korea

| Domain | Primary body | Adjacent bodies | Common source target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Game rating/classification | GRAC | MCST | Game Industry Promotion Act |
| Probability item disclosure | GRAC | MCST, KFTC | Game Industry Promotion Act and guidance |
| Youth/minor protection | MCST/MOGEF status must be verified | GRAC, KCC | Youth Protection Act, game rules |
| App-market billing | KCC | MSIT, KFTC | Telecommunications Business Act |
| App-market competition | KFTC | KCC | Fair trade statutes/decisions |
| Unfair terms/refunds | KFTC | Consumer bodies | Terms and consumer protection law |
| Privacy | PIPC | KCC where applicable | PIPA and specialist handoff |
| P2E/virtual assets | FSC/KoFIU | MCST, GRAC | Virtual-asset/financial rules |
| Esports | MCST | KeSPA | Esports promotion framework |
| Copyright/game cloning | Courts/MCST | Copyright Commission | Copyright Act |

## United States

| Domain | Federal | State/local | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Ratings | ESRB self-regulation | none typical | not a federal mandate |
| Loot boxes/gambling | FTC for consumer practices | state gambling bodies | no single federal loot-box regime |
| Children's data | FTC | state privacy laws | specialist handoff for privacy |
| App-store/antitrust | DOJ/FTC | state AGs | litigation/enforcement context matters |
| Consumer protection | FTC | state AGs | dark patterns, deceptive practices |

## European Union

| Domain | EU-level body/instrument | Member-state role |
|---|---|---|
| Data protection | GDPR, EDPB | national DPAs |
| Loot boxes/gambling | no unified EU loot-box law | national gambling regulators |
| Consumer protection | UCPD/consumer directives | national implementation/enforcement |
| Platforms | DMA/DSA where applicable | national coordinators/enforcers |
| Minors/content | AVMSD and national rules | national implementation |
